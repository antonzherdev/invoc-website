FAQ

What is AI?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a software that can solve cognitive problems used to be solved by a human.

Machine learning (ML) is a collection of methods which allow training AI on sample datasets. Similarly to human, ML can recognize patterns in the dataset and make predictions about the data it's never seen before.

How do you use AI in Invoc?

We use a combination of algorithms and machine learning to find out patterns and intelligently recognize invoices based on thousands of sample invoices. For example, we use machine learning models to find out the borders of the tables and recognize invoice lines.

Is AI dangerous?

If you think of science fictions such as Matrix or Terminator, you will be disappointed. Humankind is still far away from creating a general AI which can learn anything and make decisions on its own.

Will AI take people's jobs?

AI and robots will be gradually replacing humans in more tedious jobs. But it's not necessarily bad. It will allow people to be focused on creative jobs and probably make the working week shorter.
